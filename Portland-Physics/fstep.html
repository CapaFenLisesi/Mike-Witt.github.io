This is an animation of the "probability wave" for the Finite Step problem, as described in a typical undergraduate quantum mechanics textbook.

  • The solid grey block represents the potential step.
  • The green curve is calculated by a finite step (numerical) algorithm.
  • The black dots are the anyalytical solution.
The point of this is to gain confidence in both solutions, by animating them over time to see of they appear to be the same.
