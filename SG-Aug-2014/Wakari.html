Wakari Instructions

  1. Go to: wakari.io and make a "free" type account. You will get an email with a link that you have to click on in order to confirm the account.

  2. Once your account is set up, go to: wakari.io/mike97214

  3. There you should see a "Shared Bundle list." Click on the one you want.

  4. The next page that comes up, ignore all the warnings and click on the big green button that says "Run/Edit this Notebook"

  5. Then, after a bit, you should see a box that says "Fetch a bundle" at the top. Click the blue "Fetch" button underneath.

  6. It will do some stuff for a while, and then you'll have a screen that shows something called a "notebook" which takes up most of the display (on the right) and a narrow strip on the left that shows a list of notebooks (that end with ".ipynb") and other files.

  7. You can work with whatever notebook is already loaded on the right, or you can load another one by double clicking on its name, on the left.
